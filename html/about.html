About Tomagocha

Tomagocha was created in April of 2017 with the idea of serving the oil field community through indepth oil math calcuations. Tomagocha prides itself on being able to provide the industry with math formulas verified by experienced oilfield math educator Bon Crowder. Combined with Taylor Fienstien's geology experience, our team of four gives the industry the calculations they need, when they need them. Use our intricate and multiplex algorithm and save yourself time and money!
